Here’s a list of punchy, single-word names that work well for a new or experimental project:

Pilot – classic for a trial run.

Proto – short for prototype.

Alpha – signals early-stage testing.

Beta – slightly later-stage, still testing.

Spark – implies the start of something new.

Node – modular, tech-friendly.

Seed – growth potential.

Flux – change, experimentation.

Pulse – active, measuring, testing vibe.

Nexus – central point, experimental hub.

Edge – cutting-edge, exploratory.

Drift – trial, exploration, flexible.
